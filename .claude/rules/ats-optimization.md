# ATS Optimization Rules

The CV should include important keywords from the job posting.

Keywords should appear naturally in:

summary  
technical skills  
experience bullet points  

---

# Keyword Priority

1 required technologies  
2 domain terminology  
3 methodology terms  
4 optional tools  

---

# Restrictions

Do not repeat keywords excessively.

Do not add skills the candidate does not have.

The CV must remain readable and natural.
